My First Post - July 14th, 2016

Hi everyone! Happy Bastile Day to all of my fellow French people out there!

This past Tuesday, July 12th, we had our second meeting of CUNY Tech Prep. Part of our homework for the next six weeks is to build a website. Well here it is! Hope everyone likes it!

I actually decided to spend a lot of time yesterday working on this website because, believe it or not, I've been enjoying working on this so much. It's obviously still a work in progress because there's so much I'm trying to learn, and once I finish with the content, I'll need to step away for a bit so I can judge the design with rested eyes.

As for what I'm going to do with this blog: Well, I've never actually blogged before. Maybe I'll add pictures of something fun I've seen or done whenever I write a post. I'm open to suggestions if you think this is boring.
