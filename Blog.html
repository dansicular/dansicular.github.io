August 18th, 2016 - One Week Until Classes!

Summer is almost over, can you believe it? I can't!

With the end of the summer comes the end of my internship at UBS.

The Fall 2016 semester at Baruch may start next Thursday, but my first CTP class is this Tuesday! I'm really excited to get started.

I know it's been a while since my last blog post, but I'll try to write more as the year goes on. The whole world will get to hear about this upcoming semester.

I've decided to add a "Cheap Eats Recommendation" section to the blog. Each post I'll include a great, cheap place to eat at that I've been to recently.

This post's (and the first) Cheap Eats Recommendation:

Gowanus Yacht Club

323 Smith Street, Brooklyn, NY 11231

Get the $2 hotdog or the $6 burger (add an extra dollar or two for add-ons) made on their small backyard-style grill. Make sure you get an order of pickes too ($2). They also have various sausages that I have yet to try. It's a small place, but I like small places without music so I can hear what my friends are saying. The best part? It's outside.

Get over there now because the cold weather will be here before we know it!
