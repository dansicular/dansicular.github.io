A Warm Welcome

Some of our Hack For Baruch executive board participated in the "I am an Immigrant" Hackathon towards the end of June and we came up with "A Warm Welcome."

Our whole concept revolves around using heatmap technology to guide new immigrants to food, cultural activities, and houses of worship that would help them feel like they're in their country of origin.

I'm going to post a link to our demo website here. You can't click on most things but see what you can actually click on.

This is something I'm interested in completing once I gain the technical knowledge needed to complete it.
